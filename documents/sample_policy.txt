Vacation Policy 2024

Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation days begin accruing from the first day of employment at a rate of 1.67 days per month.

Employees must submit vacation requests at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal. Requests are approved by direct managers on a first-come, first-served basis.

Unused vacation days may be carried over to the following year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Any days beyond the carryover limit will be forfeited on January 1st.

Part-time employees receive prorated vacation based on their scheduled hours. An employee working 20 hours per week would receive 10 vacation days per year.

Sick Leave Policy 2024

All employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not accrue and resets on January 1st each year.

Employees must notify their manager before their scheduled start time if they need to take a sick day. A doctor's note is required for absences of three or more consecutive days.

Unused sick days are not paid out upon termination and cannot be converted to vacation days.
