Compensation Policy 2024

All full-time employees are paid on a bi-weekly basis, with 26 pay periods per year. Direct deposit is required for all employees.

Annual salary reviews are conducted in March of each year. Managers submit recommendations to HR by February 15th. Employees are notified of any changes by April 1st.

Merit increases are based on performance ratings. Employees rated "Exceeds Expectations" receive a 5-7% increase. Employees rated "Meets Expectations" receive a 2-4% increase. Employees rated "Below Expectations" do not receive an increase and are placed on a performance improvement plan.

Overtime is available for non-exempt employees at 1.5x their regular hourly rate for hours worked beyond 40 per week. Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager. Unauthorized overtime may result in disciplinary action.

Bonuses are distributed annually in December based on company performance and individual contribution. The target bonus for individual contributors is 10% of base salary. The target bonus for managers is 15% of base salary.
