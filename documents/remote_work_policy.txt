Remote Work Policy 2024

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Core in-office days are Tuesday and Thursday. Remote work arrangements must be documented in the HR portal.

Home office stipend: All remote-eligible employees receive a one-time $500 stipend for home office setup and $50 per month for internet reimbursement.

Employees must be available during core hours of 10am to 4pm Eastern Time regardless of location. Response time to messages during core hours should not exceed 30 minutes.

International remote work requires VP-level approval and is limited to 4 weeks per calendar year. Employees must notify HR at least 30 days before working internationally.

If an employee moves to a different state, compensation may be adjusted based on the cost of living in the new location. Employees must notify HR within 14 days of a permanent relocation.
