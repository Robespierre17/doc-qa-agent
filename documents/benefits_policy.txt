Benefits Policy 2024

Health insurance is provided through BlueCross BlueShield. The company covers 80% of premiums for employees and 50% for dependents. Open enrollment occurs in November each year.

Three plan options are available: Basic ($150/month employee cost), Standard ($250/month employee cost), and Premium ($400/month employee cost). The Premium plan includes dental and vision coverage.

401(k) retirement plan is available after 90 days of employment. The company matches 100% of contributions up to 4% of salary. Vesting is immediate for employee contributions and follows a 3-year graded schedule for employer matching.

Life insurance equal to 2x annual salary is provided at no cost to employees. Additional voluntary coverage up to 5x salary is available at group rates.

Parental leave: Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of paid leave. Secondary caregivers receive 6 weeks of paid leave. Adoption and foster placement qualify for the same benefits.

Education assistance: Employees may receive up to $5,250 per year for job-related courses and certifications. Approval from both manager and HR is required before enrollment.
